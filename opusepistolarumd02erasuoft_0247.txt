July 1516 at Tiibingen, whither he was drawn by the influence of Reuchlin.
Although not learned—he had never graduated—he enjoyed the esteem and
support of the Tiibingen humanists ; and no reason has been found to account
for his removal to Hagenau. He printed there Nov. 1516—Dec. 1522 and con-
